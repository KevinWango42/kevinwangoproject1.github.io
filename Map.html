Soccer City is located in the south-west of Johannesburg on the edge of the city of Soweto. It lies about 8 kilometres from Johannesburg’s Central Business District. Soweto city centre is about 9 kilometres further east. The venue has hosted the biggest sports and music events this country has ever seen. The stadium’s layout is very effective and easy to guide.
